Human-AI systems for continuous shopping and multimodal understanding.

I work on generative recommendation systems that shape continuous shopping experiences on Amazon.com. My research bridges LLM-based user modeling, multimodal AI, and human-AI interaction to design systems that are precise, useful, and human-centered.

About

Applied scientist with a background in HCI and multimodal AI.

Background

Trained in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Notre Dame with Prof. Toby Jia-Jun Li. Dual B.Eng. degrees in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from UESTC and the University of Glasgow.

Previously at Adobe and Microsoft as a research scientist intern.

What I Work On

  • Generative recommenders and ranking for continuous shopping journeys.
  • LLM-based user modeling and personalization at scale.
  • Multimodal understanding across visual, audio, and text.
  • Human-AI interaction and accessible, trustworthy systems.
